Goal: Give providers an easy way to review/interpret Linus test results and develop an effective care plan for their patients


Example Features:

  • Links to all test results in chronological order
  • Longitudinal test result visualizations
  • Differential diagnoses (w/ confidence level)
  • Future cognitive disease risk (w/ confidence level)
  • Recommended interventions
  • Note: getting this right is critical and deserves extra attention e.g. guidance from Alvaro, William, Advisors and usability testing with providers in the market. Further, we may need different flavors based on specialty (e.g. PCP, geriatrician, nuerologist) and/or care setting (clinic, surgical center, ICU) as goals may differ
